Harbour O' 4

This spacious lounge bar with a harbour theme offers multiple seating options and exudes a casual vibe. The bar at one end, with frenetic activity, is hard to miss. Popular among the millennials, it is the perfect spot for party revellers or those wanting to hang out with friends. The menu is exhaustive and one can look forward to American, Pan Asian and North Indian fare; abundant vegetarians options too. The 'Multani Mushrooms', 'Mutton Boti Kabab', 'Murg Sula Kabab' are a great way to start.
