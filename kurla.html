The Tanjore Tiffin Room

The Tanjore Tiffin Room is a great restaurant for a South Indian style meal. Usually, this kind of cuisine is popular in smaller no-frills restaurants but this is a great place to enjoy a meal with your family. It is a pretty space with an earthy colour scheme and lots of greenery along with potted plants hanging from the ceiling. While it gets clubbed as a South Indian restaurant, which it is, the food is more focused on Chettinad cuisine and dishes from Tamil Nadu. 'Chicken 65', 'Mini Gunpowder Idlis' and 'Yam Cutlets' are a great way to start your meal.
